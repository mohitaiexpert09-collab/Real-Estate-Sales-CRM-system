01The one-line pitch

What PropPulse is, in a sentence you can say at the door

“It’s an AI sales assistant for your property business — it answers every enquiry from 99acres, MagicBricks, Facebook and your website within seconds on WhatsApp, tells your team which leads are hot, and chases every follow-up automatically so no deal slips.”

Real-estate agencies in India don’t have a lead problem — they pay portals and run ads for plenty of leads. They have a speed and follow-up problem. A buyer enquires on three projects at once; whoever calls first usually wins. If your agent is showing a flat, at lunch, or asleep, that lead cools — and the money you spent to get it is wasted. PropPulse closes that gap with software instead of hiring more people.

02Why they’ll pay

The problem we sell against

Three leaks quietly drain every agency. PropPulse plugs all three — and that’s the whole ROI story.

Leak 01

Slow first response

The average portal lead waits hours for a callback. Buyers move on to the developer or broker who replied in the first minute. Ad money spent, lead lost.

Leak 02

Dropped follow-ups

“I’ll call them tomorrow” becomes never. Warm leads sit in a WhatsApp scroll or a notebook and go cold with zero system to chase them.

Leak 03

No idea who’s hot

Agents treat every enquiry the same, spending time on window-shoppers while a ready, loan-approved buyer waits in the same list.

One extra booking a year pays for the whole system — several times over.

A single ₹1 crore flat earns the agency roughly ₹2–5 lakh in brokerage. PropPulse costs a one-time setup plus a modest monthly fee. If it rescues even one deal that would otherwise have leaked, it has already paid for a year. Everything after that is margin. That is the entire sales argument — you’re not selling software, you’re selling recovered bookings.

06Under the hood (plain language)

What it’s built on — and what “phase 2” means

You don’t need to be technical to sell this, but here’s the honest picture so you’re never caught out. PropPulse is a modern web app — it runs in any browser, nothing to install for the client. It’s built on Next.js (the same framework behind many production apps), with a clean database underneath and charts drawn with a proper data-viz library. The AI features call OpenAI or Claude live.

In the demo, WhatsApp is shown as a realistic in-app chat thread and leads are seeded with realistic Mumbai / Pune / Bangalore projects and ₹ budgets — so every screen looks live from the first second. When a client signs, phase 2 is the paid integration work that makes it their real business:

Phase 2

Real WhatsApp

Connect an official WhatsApp Business number via a provider (AiSensy, Gupshup, Meta Cloud API or Twilio) so replies go to real buyers.

Phase 2

Portal & ad feeds

Live hooks from 99acres, MagicBricks, Housing and Facebook Lead Ads so enquiries flow in automatically, not by hand.

Phase 2

Their data & team

Import their existing leads, add each agent with a login, and brand the whole system with the agency’s name and colours.

The honest framing for clients: what you’re showing today is the complete working product on demo data. Setup is where we connect it to their WhatsApp, their portals and their team — that’s the value in the setup fee, and it’s deliberately not a five-minute job.

10Be ready for these

Handling the questions they’ll ask

“Is this just a chatbot?”
No. A chatbot only talks. PropPulse acts — it scores leads, sends replies, moves deals, books visits, and gives the owner live numbers. It’s a full sales system with AI staff inside it.
“Will it really message on WhatsApp?”
Yes, on an official WhatsApp Business number we connect during setup via a licensed provider. In the demo it’s shown as a chat thread so you can see exactly how it behaves.
“We already have a CRM.”
Most CRMs are just a database people forget to update. Ask: “Does yours reply to a lead in 45 seconds and follow up on its own?” That’s the difference — this one does the work, it doesn’t just store it.
“What does it cost to run?”
The monthly fee covers WhatsApp messaging, AI usage and support. It’s a small, predictable number against the brokerage on a single deal — and we size it to their volume.
“How long to set up?”
The product is ready today. Setup is connecting their WhatsApp, portals, team and data — typically a short, guided onboarding, which is what the setup fee covers.
